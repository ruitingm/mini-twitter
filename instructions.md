Problem, Team, and Overview of Experiments
==========================================
Describe the problem you want to solve, and why it is important for your future stakeholdres.  Introduce each team member, and what they bring to the team. Also identify the kinds of results your experiements will be evaluating. 

Project Plan and Recent Progress
================================
Describe your recent progress in activities related to the proposal.  This should include a timeline and breakdown of tasks, in addition to an overview of "who is doing what" in terms of the project plan you have created.

Objectives
==========
Define the short- and long-term objectives of your project. Note that a project should have a long-term vision that expands beyond the course. This most likely means that you will be identifying future work in a concrete way.

Related work
============
Discuss all of the related work pertinent to the project, placing the proposed project in the context of the this work.  This should include readings from the course, but can also include any/all ouside material that you find useful.

Methodology
===========
Describe the methods and proposed approach, providing sufficient details to allow a reader to assess the feasibility of the proposed activities.  Provide a detailed overview of the experiments you will be performing.

Preliminary Results
===================
For each experiment you have designed, include all preliminary results you have collected.  Identify what is left to collect/analyze to finish these results for the project. 

Impact
======
Explain the anticipated significance of the work.  Why are people going to see this project and care about your results?
